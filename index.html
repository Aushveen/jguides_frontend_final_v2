About J - Guide

My name is Josh Aronow, and I am a 2024 graduate of Indiana University's Kelley School of Business. The inspiration for J-Guides stemmed from my own experiences navigating the complexities of freshman year. I often wished for the advice, mentorship, and insights from someone who had already been through the process. I firmly believe that guidance from a relatable graduate can significantly impact a student’s college journey.

Freshman year is often filled with confusion and the overwhelming task of adapting to a new environment. My guidebook aims to alleviate these challenges by providing essential information, resources, and mentorship for both students and parents, all in one comprehensive package.
