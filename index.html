About J-GUIDES

My name is Josh Aronow, and I am a 2024 graduate from Indiana University's Kelley School of Business. The inspiration for J-Guides came from my experiences navigating the complexities of freshman year. I wished I had the advice, mentorship, and knowledge from a graduate before coming to IU. I believe that such guidance can significantly impact a student, shaping them into a professional and a well-rounded individual. We all remember the confusion and the overwhelming feeling of having so much to learn in a new place. My guidebook is designed to alleviate that confusion, providing essential information and much more, all in one place.
